RFP — Purchasing Transformation Program

ACME Aerospace (Tier-1 aerostructures supplier, ~2,400 employees, plants in
Toulouse and Hamburg) invites proposals for a purchasing transformation
program.

Context: input costs have risen 18% over two years and the supplier base is
fragmented across 1,100 active vendors. The purchasing organisation (35 FTE,
Function: Purchasing) operates without commodity strategies or a savings
tracking process.

Scope requested:
1. Assess the current purchasing organisation, processes and spend baseline.
2. Design commodity strategies for the top 8 commodity families.
3. Identify and commit a savings pipeline, with a tracking mechanism.
4. Define the target operating model and implementation roadmap.

Expected deliverables: as-is assessment, spend cube, commodity strategy
playbooks, savings roadmap with committed targets, TOM and transition plan.

Timeline: 12 weeks, starting September 2026. Proposal language: English.
Please include relevant credentials in aerospace & defense procurement and
CVs of the proposed team.
